Welcome to the Amstelcampus

The Amstelcampus of the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences is a vibrant and dynamic area in the heart of Amsterdam. It offers modern facilities and spaces where students can study, exercise, and engage in social activities. Visit the campus and discover it for yourself!

Amstelcampus - HvA's Beautiful Locations

The Amstelcampus is one of the most beautiful spots at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. It combines modern architecture with green spaces, providing students with an inspiring environment to study.

Amstelcampus - Library

The library at the Amstelcampus is a great place to study, conduct research, and access an extensive collection of books, articles, and digital resources.

Amstelcampus - Study Rooms

The Amstelcampus offers various study rooms, ideal for both individual study and group meetings. You can book in advance to secure a room.

Amstelcampus - Sports Facilities

At the Amstelcampus, you can use modern sports facilities. Whether you want to work out, join a group class, or simply relax after your studies.
